## Data, Bioinformatics & Analysis

MicroMosaic approaches biological data as living context. Every analysis includes interpretation, collaboration with wet-lab partners, and figures ready for manuscripts, decks, or stakeholders.

**Representative work**

- Whole genome, metagenomic, amplicon, and RNA-seq pipelines for bacteria and fungi
- Comparative genomics, phylogenetics, and multi-omics synthesis
- Experimental design guidance, metadata strategy, and QC planning
- Visual and narrative reporting that makes decisions clear

**Collaboration looks like** discovery calls, scoped analyses, and iterative interpretation sessions. Pipelines can be handed off or run as a managed service depending on team capacity.

## Science Communication & Translation

Communication is participatory, not performative. MicroMosaic turns complex results into narratives that maintain accuracy while inviting curiosity.

**Representative work**

- Scientific writing for technical, mixed, and public audiences
- Podcast and audio development, interviews, and sponsored storytelling
- Editorial support for journals, manuscripts, SOPs, and grant narratives
- Workshops on data literacy, interpretation, and collaborative sense-making

**Collaboration looks like** co-developing outlines, scripts, or briefs with your SMEs, layering metaphor only when it clarifies, and providing research-backed storytelling assets.
